Workshops We Offer

Our focus is to provide accessible technology for students & developers. We want engineering students to get familiarized with applications of the Internet of things under the guidance of eminent speakers from ISRO, HCU, Infosys and Osmania University. This can encourage students to explore the vast possibilities and create something new.

Internet of Things
NodeMCU Workshop

This project-based course introduces the concept of the Internet of Things and its exciting applications. This workshop deals with Python basics and getting started with a NodeMCU board. Hands-on projects like temperature & humidity sensing and cloud-based realtime messaging are included.

Kits will be given to students to take home. Certification will be provided at the end of the course.

This workshop gives a glimpse of how integrating Embedded systems and Internet can change our daily lives! It covers Python basics and getting started with Raspberry Pi. Hands-on projects involves IoT system architecture, realtime publish/subscribe model and cloud data visualization.
